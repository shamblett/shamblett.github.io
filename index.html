About Me, Dart and everything

Hi all Dartisans, this my starter site for what will hopefully become a fuller dive into the Dartiverse.


I've been in the Dartiverse since the start of Dart 1.0, my first published package, xml2json, was back in September 2013. I've seen a few changes here and there and picked up a lot of Dart knowledge along the way. I mainly stay on the server side of things in the comfort of the VM though I have a few both server and web packages.


Lately I've been writing a few Medium articles about Dart, look for Steve Hamblett(stevehamblett) on Medium


You can mail me at steve.hamblett@linux.com on any Dart topics, especially server side development.

You can also get me in the fediverse at @shamblett@darticulate.com.


I have a particular interest in using Dart in the IOT world, a write up for starter project using Google's IOT-Core cloud platform is here, however I have many years knowledge of software development in high end industrial SCADA and Plant Management and Control systems, interfacing to devices/sensors is all part and parcel of this so feel free to ping me ony any questions you may have in this area, Dart related or not.


Happy Darting!
